# Module 27 Reflection — Latin in Science

Module Reflection wrapper: one question only, delivered per lane as referenced in each lesson's "What You'll Do Next."

## Foundations (K-2) — Spoken or Drawn

**Format:** Canvas Assignment (completion-based), not typed. Student responds by talking to a grown-up or by drawing, per the untimed/no-independent-reading rule for this lane.

**Prompt:** "Tell a grown-up or draw: if you got to give an animal a two-word Latin name, what animal would you pick and what would you want its name to mean?"

**Grading:** Completion-based only. Marked complete when the student (or a grown-up on the student's behalf) confirms the reflection happened — spoken aloud or drawn. No content scoring.

## Primary (3-5) — Typed

**Format:** Canvas Assignment (short text-entry), 1-2 sentences typed by the student.

**Prompt:** "Why do you think scientists use Latin and Greek names instead of just English names for animals?"

**Grading:** Completion/effort-based per standard Module Reflection convention — checked for a genuine 1-2 sentence attempt, not for a single "correct" answer.
