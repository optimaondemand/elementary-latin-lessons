One last look back before the celebration
This is it — the final review of the year! Today you'll revisit gods, daily life, and the engineering and ideas Rome left behind, all in one big cumulative game. Then it's time for the best part of the whole year: opening your Roman Passport one last time and showing off everything you've learned.

2

📜 PART 2 · READING: A YEAR'S JOURNEY THROUGH ROME
This year you met the gods Romans believed watched over sky and sea, love and wisdom, hearth and home. You stepped inside a Roman familia's domus, imagined market bread and a well-worn toga, and traced the viae and aquaeductūs that once carried travelers and water across an entire empire. Word by word, you found pieces of Rome's legacy still living inside English, inside science, and inside ideas about government that people use even now. Before this year closes, it's time to open your Roman Passport one last time and show how far you've traveled.
