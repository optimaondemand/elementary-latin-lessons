📖 TODAY'S FOCUS
Let's remember everything we learned about counting, days, and months in Latin this year!
You have learned so many Latin words this year! Today we bring them all back together — counting numbers, naming the days of the week, and saying the months of the year. You won't have to remember every single one today. We'll just visit some old friends and see how much you already know.
🎯 BY THE END OF TODAY YOU WILL BE ABLE TO…
1
I can say some Latin numbers I remember.
2
I can name a Latin day of the week.
3
I can name a Latin month of the year.
📋 WHAT YOU NEED
▶
Headphones or speakers
▶
A grown-up nearby is helpful but not required
⚡ PART 1 · LET'S BEGIN
⚡ QUICK CHECK-IN
Which do you think you'll remember best today: numbers, days, or months?

Say each word out loud with me. See which ones you still remember!

▶  Go Further: count as high as you can with a grown-up
Ask a grown-up to count with you in Latin, as high as you both can remember — ūnus, duo, trēs, and beyond! See how far you get together.
