## Foundations (K-2)

**Format:** Spoken or drawn — not typed, not graded beyond completion. A parent/facilitator may note that the student completed it.

**Prompt:** "Draw or tell a grown-up: if you were in charge of a class meeting, what's one thing you and your class would decide together?"

## Primary (3-5)

**Format:** Typed, 1-2 sentences.

**Prompt:** "In your own words, explain why the idea of a rēspūblica — a 'public thing' that belonged to everyone, not just to one king — might have mattered to the Romans."
