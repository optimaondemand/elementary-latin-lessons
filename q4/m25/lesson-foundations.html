📖 TODAY'S FOCUS
A place, a group, and a big idea from long ago
Long ago in Rome, there was a giant outdoor square called the forum where everyone gathered. There was also a group of Roman leaders called the senātus (Senate) who met together and took turns talking about what was best for the city. And there was a big idea called rēspūblica — that the city belonged to everyone, not just to one king. Let's meet all three today!
🎯 BY THE END OF TODAY YOU WILL BE ABLE TO…
1
I can point to the picture of the forum, the giant outdoor meeting place.
2
I can tell what the Roman Senate did.
3
I can tell a grown-up why the city belonged to everyone.
