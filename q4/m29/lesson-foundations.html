📖 TODAY'S FOCUS
Two heroes from old Roman stories!
Today we meet two heroes from old Roman stories! One is famous for being super strong. The other went on a very long trip across the sea. Let's find out who they are.
🎯 BY THE END OF TODAY YOU WILL BE ABLE TO…
1
I can tell about Hercules and how strong and brave he was.
2
I can tell about Aeneas and his long trip across the sea to Italy.
3
I can share which hero's story I liked best.
📋 WHAT YOU NEED
▶
Headphones or speakers
▶
A grown-up nearby is helpful but not required
⚡ PART 1 · LET'S BEGIN
⚡ QUICK CHECK-IN
Have you ever heard a story about a super strong hero before?
🦸 PART 2 · MEET THE HEROES

Long ago, Roman families told stories about heroes. Here are two of them.

🦁
Hercules
the strong hero who fought a lion and a scary many-headed serpent!
⛵
Aeneas
the hero who sailed across the sea all the way to Italy!
👋 PART 3 · WHICH HERO IS IT?

Now you try: which hero matches which picture?

⚡ TRY IT · MATCH THE HERO
Tap the hero that matches the picture.
🦁 (the fierce lion)
⛵ (a ship sailing across the sea)
🤗 NEED HELP?
Two new heroes is plenty for one day! Look at the picture cards again — Hercules is with the lion, and Aeneas is with the ship. You'll hear about both of them again.
▶  Go Further: look at hero pictures with a grown-up
If you'd like, ask a grown-up to help you find a picture of Hercules or Aeneas online or in a book. Old Roman heroes were drawn and painted for hundreds and hundreds of years!
🎮 EXTRA PRACTICE · PLAY & EXPLORE

Let's play! Drag each hero to what they did. Play as many times as you like!

➡️ WHAT YOU'LL DO NEXT
1
Module 29 Quick Check
A fun picture-matching game with Hercules and Aeneas. Play it as many times as you like!
2
Tell a Grown-Up or Draw
Tell a grown-up or draw a picture: which hero's story did you like better today, and why? That's your reflection!
💪 GREAT START

You just met two heroes from old Roman stories — Hercules the strong, and Aeneas the sailor! We'll keep exploring Rome's legacy together soon.
