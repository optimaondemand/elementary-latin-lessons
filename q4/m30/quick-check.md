## Foundations (K-2) — Canvas Assignment (Completion-Based)

**Type:** Canvas Assignment, graded on completion only (no auto-scoring, no rubric). Tier 1 — Student and Material, 0 teacher hours.

**Title:** Module 30 Quick Check — Big God-Matching Review

**Directions to student (plain language):**
"You've met eleven Roman gods and goddesses this year! Play the matching game below and sort each god's picture next to their symbol. Take your time — you can play as many times as you like. When you're done, mark this assignment complete."

**Activity description:**
A cumulative, untimed picture-matching/sorting activity reusing the "God-Matching Tournament" widget content from the Foundations lesson (Part 3): a subset of 5 memorable god-to-symbol pairs (Jupiter/lightning bolt, Neptune/trident, Mercury/winged sandals, Vulcan/hammer-forge, Diana/moon-and-bow), presented as drag-to-sort or tap-to-match picture cards. No reading required — all prompts are pictures/emoji plus a single god name label per card.

**Grading:** Completion-based only (submitted/not submitted, or "marked as done"). No correct/incorrect scoring recorded in the gradebook — the in-widget feedback (built into the lesson) already tells the student right away whether their match was correct.

**Submission type:** No file submission required; a "mark as done" completion requirement, or a simple Canvas Assignment with an embedded link back to the lesson's Part 3 widget for replay, satisfies this.

## Primary (3-5) — Canvas Quiz (Auto-Graded, 5 Questions)

**Type:** Canvas Quiz, auto-graded, multiple choice. Tier 1 — Student and Material, 0 teacher hours. Best of 2 attempts (matches lesson's "What You'll Do Next" description).

**Title:** Module 30 Quick Check — Gods of Rome Full Review

### Question 1
Which god is known as the messenger god, associated with travel and commerce, and is shown wearing winged sandals?

A) Apollo
B) Mercury — **CORRECT**
C) Mars
D) Vulcan

Feedback (correct): Correct! Mercury is the messenger god, linked to travel and commerce, and shown with winged sandals.

### Question 2
Which goddess is Jupiter's wife, known as the queen of the gods and the goddess of marriage and women?

A) Venus
B) Diana
C) Juno — **CORRECT**
D) Vesta

Feedback (correct): Correct! Juno is Jupiter's wife, queen of the gods, and goddess of marriage and women.

### Question 3
Which god is associated with the sun, music, and prophecy?

A) Apollo — **CORRECT**
B) Mars
C) Neptune
D) Vulcan

Feedback (correct): Correct! Apollo is the god of the sun, music, and prophecy.

### Question 4
Which goddess is associated with the hearth and home?

A) Minerva
B) Vesta — **CORRECT**
C) Venus
D) Diana

Feedback (correct): Correct! Vesta is the goddess of the hearth and home.

### Question 5
Which god rules over the sea, and carries the trident as his symbol?

A) Jupiter
B) Mars
C) Neptune — **CORRECT**
D) Mercury

Feedback (correct): Correct! Neptune is the god of the sea, and his symbol is the trident.

**Sourcing note:** All five questions and their correct answers draw only from the verified, standard Roman mythology domains supplied for this module (Jupiter, Juno, Neptune, Mars, Venus, Minerva, Mercury, Apollo, Diana, Vulcan, Vesta). No additional myths, stories, or attributes were introduced.
