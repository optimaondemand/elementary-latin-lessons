📖 TODAY'S FOCUS
Bringing all eleven gods and goddesses back together
Across this year you've built up a whole cast of Roman gods, three or four at a time. Today is a full cumulative review: every god you've met, in one place, so you can see how the whole pantheon fits together before we move further into Rome's legacy.
🎯 BY THE END OF THIS LESSON YOU WILL BE ABLE TO…
1
I can name all eleven Roman gods and goddesses introduced so far this year.
2
I can match each god to their domain and, where there is one, their symbol.
3
I can explain why Romans linked certain gods with certain powers, like Neptune with the sea.

🏛️ PART 2 · READING: A REUNION OF THE GODS
This year you have met eleven gods and goddesses of Rome, a few at a time. In Module 8, you were introduced to Jupiter, Juno, and Neptune, the great rulers of sky, marriage, and sea. In Module 15, you met Mars, Venus, Minerva, and Mercury. In Module 21, you met Apollo, Diana, Vulcan, and Vesta. Today, all eleven come back together for one full review.
