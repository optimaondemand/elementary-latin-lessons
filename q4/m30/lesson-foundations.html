📖 TODAY'S FOCUS
A big reunion of every Roman god we've met this year!
This year you met eleven gods and goddesses of Rome. Today they all come back together for one big review! You don't have to remember every single one perfectly — just have fun saying hello to old friends and seeing how many symbols you recognize.
🎯 BY THE END OF TODAY YOU WILL BE ABLE TO…
1
I can look at pictures of all eleven Roman gods and goddesses we've met this year.
2
I can match a few gods to their special symbol, like a lightning bolt or a trident.
3
I can tell a grown-up or draw a picture of my favorite Roman god from this year.
📋 WHAT YOU NEED
▶
A grown-up nearby is helpful but not required
▶
Paper and crayons, in case you want to draw
⚡ PART 1 · LET'S BEGIN
⚡ QUICK CHECK-IN
Which Roman symbol do you remember best?

🏆 PART 3 · BIG REVIEW MATCHING GAME

Let's play a matching tournament with a few of the symbols above. Look at the picture, then tap the god it belongs to!

🤗 NEED HELP?
Eleven gods is a lot to remember at once! It's perfectly okay to focus on just a few symbols today. Scroll back up to Part 2 any time you want another look at the pictures.
▶  Go Further: quiz a grown-up back!
Point to a picture in Part 2 and ask a grown-up to guess the god's name. See if you know more about Roman gods than they do!

You just said hello again to all eleven gods and goddesses of Rome! You've learned so much about Rome this year. Get ready for more of Rome's legacy still to come.
