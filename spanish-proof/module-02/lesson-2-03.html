Lesson 2.03
Words you already know, sorted by gender
New grammar (gender) on words you've met before — nothing new to memorize.
📖 TODAY'S FOCUS
Let's apply yesterday's rule to ten Spanish words you can already recognize.
Every word below is a Spanish word that English borrowed — you met most of them in Module 1. Because you already know the words, your only job today is the new part: hearing them, and deciding el or la using the -o/-a pattern.
🎯 BY THE END OF THIS LESSON YOU WILL BE ABLE TO…
1
say ten common Spanish loanwords with clean Spanish vowels.
2
sort each one into el or la using its ending.
