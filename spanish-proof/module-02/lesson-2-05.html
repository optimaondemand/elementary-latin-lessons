📚 THE WHOLE MODULE IN THREE CARDS

1 · Gender exists

English has one "the." Spanish sorts every noun into masculine or feminine, and "the" changes to match: el or la.

2 · Read the ending

Ends in -o → usually el. Ends in -a → usually la. A few exceptions exist (el día, la mano, el mapa) — just recognize them.

3 · Cognates are free

Thousands of Spanish words are cousins of English words (both borrowed from Latin), so you understand them instantly — la familia, la música, el animal.
