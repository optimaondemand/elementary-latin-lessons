# Module 24 Reflection — Roman Army Basics

---

## Foundations (K-2) — Spoken Reflection

**Type:** Canvas Assignment — one-question, spoken response. A parent/facilitator observes and notes the response; no typing required from the student.

**Prompt:** "Tell a grown-up why the Roman army needed to stay organized."

**Facilitator note field (optional):** A short line for the grown-up to note what the student said and whether they needed a picture reminder from the lesson.

**Grading:** Completion only.

---

## Primary (3-5) — Typed Reflection

**Type:** Canvas Assignment — short typed response (1-2 sentences).

**Prompt:** "Which part of the Roman army's organization did you find most surprising today, and why?"

**Grading:** Completion / effort-based. No right or wrong answer.
