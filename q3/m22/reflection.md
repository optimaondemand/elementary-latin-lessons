# Module 22 Reflection — Everyday Latin Phrases

---

## Foundations (K-2) — Spoken/Drawn Reflection

**Type:** Canvas Assignment — one-question, spoken or drawn response. A parent/facilitator observes and notes the response; no typing required from the student.

**Prompt:** "Point to a list, a school, or think about a meeting, and say one of today's Latin words out loud to a grown-up — *et cētera, alma māter, agenda,* or *vēto*."

**Facilitator note field (optional):** A short line for the grown-up to note which word the student used and whether they needed a picture reminder.

**Grading:** Completion only.

---

## Primary (3-5) — Typed Reflection

**Type:** Canvas Assignment — short typed response (1-2 sentences).

**Prompt:** "Which of today's four Latin phrases (et cētera, alma māter, agenda, vēto) do you think you'll notice first now that you know it, and where do you expect to see or hear it?"

**Grading:** Completion / effort-based. No right or wrong answer.
