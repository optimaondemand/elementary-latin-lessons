📖 TODAY'S FOCUS
Some Roman words never went away!
Nobody speaks Latin every day anymore — but a few Roman words are still hard at work! Grown-ups use them at school, in meetings, on lists, and even when a leader says no to a new law. Today we meet four of these words.
