📖 TODAY'S FOCUS
A school called a lūdus!
Long, long ago, Roman children went to a small school called a lūdus. Their teacher was called a magister, and each student was called a discipulus. Today we say these three words, and we look at how Roman school tools compare to the ones you use now!
🎯 BY THE END OF TODAY YOU WILL BE ABLE TO…
1
I can say three Latin words: lūdus, discipulus, magister.
2
I can point to the school tool we use today that matches a Roman school tool.
3
I can show a grown-up one Latin school word I learned.

🔁 PART 3 · SCHOOL TOOLS: THEN AND NOW

A Roman discipulus did not have the same tools you have. Which tool do we use today instead of each Roman tool?

🤗 NEED HELP?

▶  Go Further: pretend to be a Roman discipulus
Grab a piece of paper and pretend it is a wax tablet. Practice writing one letter, just like a Roman discipulus would have practiced on a wax tablet with a stylus.
