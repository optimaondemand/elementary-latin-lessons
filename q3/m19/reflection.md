# Module 19 Reflection — School in Rome

---

## Foundations (K-2) — Spoken/Drawn Reflection

**Type:** Canvas Assignment — one-question, spoken or drawn response. A parent/facilitator observes and notes the response; no typing required from the student.

**Prompt:** "Tell a grown-up one Latin school word you learned today — *lūdus*, *discipulus*, or *magister* — and what it means. You can also draw a picture of a Roman school tool instead."

**Facilitator note field (optional):** A short line for the grown-up to note which word (or tool) the student chose and whether they needed a picture reminder.

**Grading:** Completion only.

---

## Primary (3-5) — Typed Reflection

**Type:** Canvas Assignment — short typed response (1-2 sentences).

**Prompt:** "What was one way a day at a Roman lūdus was different from your school day today?"

**Grading:** Completion / effort-based. No right or wrong answer.
