# Module 18 Reflection — The Body in Latin

---

## Foundations (K-2) — Spoken/Drawn Reflection

**Type:** Canvas Assignment — one-question, spoken or drawn response. A parent/facilitator observes and notes the response; no typing required from the student.

**Prompt:** "Point to a body part and tell a grown-up its Latin name — *corpus*, *manus*, *pēs*, or *oculus*."

**Facilitator note field (optional):** A short line for the grown-up to note which body word the student used and whether they needed a picture reminder.

**Grading:** Completion only.

---

## Primary (3-5) — Typed Reflection

**Type:** Canvas Assignment — short typed response (1-2 sentences).

**Prompt:** "Which Latin body word was easiest for you to remember today, and why? (For example: did it sound like an English word you already knew?)"

**Grading:** Completion / effort-based. No right or wrong answer.
