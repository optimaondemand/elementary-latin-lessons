# Module 23 Reflection — Pompeii: A City Frozen in Time

---

## Foundations (K-2) — Spoken/Drawn Reflection

**Type:** Canvas Assignment — one-question, spoken or drawn response. A parent/facilitator observes and notes the response; no typing required from the student.

**Prompt:** "Draw the mountain keeping the city safe underneath, like a time capsule, and tell a grown-up about your picture."

**Facilitator note field (optional):** A short line for the grown-up to note what the student drew and whether they could describe the "time capsule" idea in their own words.

**Grading:** Completion only.

---

## Primary (3-5) — Typed Reflection

**Type:** Canvas Assignment — short typed response (1-2 sentences).

**Prompt:** "Which artifact found at Pompeii do you think best shows what daily life was like for a Roman family, and why?"

**Grading:** Completion / effort-based. No right or wrong answer.
