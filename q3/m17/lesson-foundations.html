📖 TODAY'S FOCUS
Roman floors made of a thousand tiny colored pieces!
Romans decorated their floors with pictures made from hundreds of tiny colored stones and glass, fitted together like a puzzle. This is called a mosaic. Today we learn the Latin names for four colors Romans used all the time, and then we build our own mosaic!
🎯 BY THE END OF TODAY YOU WILL BE ABLE TO…
1
I can say four Latin color words: ruber, albus, niger, viridis.
2
I can point to the color that matches each Latin word.
3
I can build a simple mosaic pattern using the right color names.

🟥 PART 3 · FOUR LATIN COLORS

Say these color words out loud with me!

ruber
"red"
albus
"white"
niger
"black"
viridis
"green"

Now you try: which word matches which colored square?

⚡ TRY IT · MATCH THE COLOR
Tap the word that matches the square.

🤗 NEED HELP?
Four colors is a lot to remember at once! Look back at the color blocks above and say each word out loud a few times before you try again.
▶  Go Further: find these colors around your house
Look around your room. Can you find something ruber, something albus, something niger, and something viridis? Point to each one and say the Latin word.
