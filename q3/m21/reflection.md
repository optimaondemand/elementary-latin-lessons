# Module 21 Reflection — Gods of Rome III

---

## Foundations (K-2) — Spoken/Drawn Reflection

**Type:** Canvas Assignment — one-question, spoken or drawn response. A parent/facilitator observes and notes the response; no typing required from the student.

**Prompt:** "Draw or point to your favorite symbol from today — the lyre, the bow and arrow, the hammer, or the hearth fire — and tell a grown-up which god it belongs to."

**Facilitator note field (optional):** A short line for the grown-up to note which symbol and god the student chose and whether they needed a picture reminder.

**Grading:** Completion only.

---

## Primary (3-5) — Typed Reflection

**Type:** Canvas Assignment — short typed response (1-2 sentences).

**Prompt:** "Which of today's four gods — Apollō, Diāna, Vulcānus, or Vesta — do you find most interesting, and why?"

**Grading:** Completion / effort-based. No right or wrong answer.
