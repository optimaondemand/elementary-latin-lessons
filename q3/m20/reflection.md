# Module 20 Reflection — Weather & Seasons

---

## Foundations (K-2) — Spoken/Drawn Reflection

**Type:** Canvas Assignment — one-question, spoken or drawn response. A parent/facilitator observes and notes the response; no typing required from the student.

**Prompt:** "Tell a grown-up which season it is right now, using the Latin word — *vēr*, *aestās*, *hiems*, or *autumnus*."

**Facilitator note field (optional):** A short line for the grown-up to note which season word the student used and whether they needed a picture reminder.

**Grading:** Completion only.

---

## Primary (3-5) — Typed Reflection

**Type:** Canvas Assignment — short typed response (1-2 sentences).

**Prompt:** "Which Latin season word do you think is easiest for you to remember, and why? (For example: does it sound like an English word you already know?)"

**Grading:** Completion / effort-based. No right or wrong answer.
