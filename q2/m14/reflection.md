# Module 14 Reflection — The Colosseum & Roman Entertainment

## Foundations (K-2) — Spoken/Drawn Reflection
**Type:** Canvas Assignment (facilitator-noted, completion-based)
**Prompt (read aloud to student):** "Draw or tell a grown-up: would you rather watch the games in the huge stone stadium, or watch a chariot race at the Circus Maximus? Why?"
**How it's captured:** A parent or facilitator either jots down a short note of the child's spoken answer directly on the Canvas assignment (as a comment or attached text), or takes a photo of the child's drawing and attaches it to the submission. No correctness grade is applied — the assignment is marked complete once a spoken answer or drawing has been captured.

## Primary (3-5) — Typed Reflection
**Type:** Canvas Assignment, text entry, completion or light credit
**Prompt:** "In one or two sentences, explain why the Colosseum was such an impressive engineering achievement for its time."
**Expected length:** 1-2 sentences
