# Module 11 Reflection — Months & the Calendar

## Foundations (K-2) — Spoken/Drawn Reflection
**Type:** Canvas Assignment (facilitator-noted, completion-based)
**Prompt (read aloud to student):** "Draw a picture of your favorite month, and tell a grown-up its Latin name out loud."
**How it's captured:** A parent or facilitator jots a one-line note in the Canvas assignment comment (or checks a completion box) confirming the student drew a month and attempted to say its Latin name aloud. No correctness judgment is needed — any sincere attempt counts as complete. The drawing itself can be photographed and attached if the family wishes, but this is optional.

## Primary (3-5) — Typed Reflection
**Type:** Canvas Assignment, text entry, completion or light credit
**Prompt:** "In one or two sentences, explain in your own words why October's name comes from the Latin word for eight (octō) even though it's now our tenth month."
**Expected length:** 1-2 sentences
