# Module 16 Reflection — Animals of Rome

## Foundations (K-2) — Spoken/Drawn Reflection
**Type:** Canvas Assignment (facilitator-noted, completion-based)
**Prompt (read aloud to student):** "What surprised you about the Cave Canem dog sign? Draw it or tell a grown-up about it."
**How it's captured:** A parent or facilitator either jots down the student's spoken answer in the Canvas Assignment comment box, or the student draws their own version of a "Beware of Dog" picture and a grown-up snaps a quick photo to upload as the submission. Completion is all that's required; there is no correctness grade.

## Primary (3-5) — Typed Reflection
**Type:** Canvas Assignment, text entry, completion or light credit
**Prompt:** "What surprised you about the Cave Canem mosaic?"
**Expected length:** 1-2 sentences
