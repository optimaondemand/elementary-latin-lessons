📖 TODAY'S FOCUS
Every Roman god had a picture that stood for them.
You already met Jupiter, Juno, and Neptune. Today you'll meet four more Roman gods — and each one has a special symbol that helps you remember who they are, just like Jupiter's eagle or Neptune's trident.
🎯 BY THE END OF TODAY YOU WILL BE ABLE TO…
1
I can name four more Roman gods: Mārs, Venus, Minerva, and Mercurius.
2
I can match each god's picture symbol to their name.
3
I can tell a grown-up my favorite symbol from today.
📋 WHAT YOU NEED
▶
Headphones or speakers
▶
A grown-up nearby is helpful but not required

⚡ QUICK CHECK-IN
