# Module 15 Reflection — Gods of Rome II

## Foundations (K-2) — Spoken/Drawn Reflection
**Type:** Canvas Assignment (facilitator-noted, completion-based)
**Prompt (read aloud to student):** "Which of today's symbols is your favorite — the spear and shield, the dove, the owl, or the winged sandals? Draw it or tell a grown-up about it."
**How it's captured:** A parent or facilitator either jots down the student's spoken answer in the Canvas Assignment comment box, or the student draws the chosen symbol on paper/whiteboard and a grown-up snaps a quick photo to upload as the submission. Completion is all that's required; there is no correctness grade.

## Primary (3-5) — Typed Reflection
**Type:** Canvas Assignment, text entry, completion or light credit
**Prompt:** "Which of the seven gods' symbols is easiest for you to remember, and why?"
**Expected length:** 1-2 sentences
