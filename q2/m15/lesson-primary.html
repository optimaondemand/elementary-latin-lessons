🏛️ PART 2 · READING: FOUR MORE GODS
Mārs was the Roman god of war. Romans pictured him with a spear and shield. Venus, goddess of love and beauty, was pictured with a gentle dove. Minerva, goddess of wisdom and crafts, was shown with a wise owl and an olive branch. Mercurius, god of travel, messages, and trade, wore winged sandals so he could move quickly between places.
