# Module 15 Quick Check — Gods of Rome II

**Teacher note (not shown to students):** CPALMS NM.6.4, NH.2.2

## Foundations (K-2) — Canvas Assignment (completion-based)
**Type:** Canvas Assignment, completion grading only (no correctness grade)
**Title:** Match the God's Symbol — Round 2
**Instructions shown to student:** Tap or point to the picture that matches each god's name. Play as many times as you like — there's no clock and no wrong way to practice!
**Activity description:** A picture-matching activity (built as a Canvas Assignment with an embedded interactive, or a facilitator-led matching activity using printed/on-screen picture cards) that presents all seven gods met so far this year — Iuppiter (eagle & thunderbolt), Iūnō (peacock), Neptūnus (trident), Mārs (spear & shield), Venus (dove), Minerva (owl & olive branch), and Mercurius (winged sandals) — one at a time, with 3 picture-symbol choices per god (1 correct, 2 distractors drawn from other gods' symbols so no symbol is guessable by elimination alone). No reading of Latin text is required to complete it; the god's name may be spoken aloud by a narrator/audio cue alongside the picture choices. Untimed. A teacher building this in Canvas can either embed the lesson's `.ow-select` picture-matching widget or use a simple drag-and-drop/matching Canvas Assignment template.
**Submission / completion criteria:** Marked complete when the student has attempted a match for all seven gods, regardless of number of tries. No score is recorded beyond completion.

**Type:** Canvas Quiz, multiple choice, auto-graded
**Title:** Module 15 Quick Check: Gods of Rome II
