# Module 02 Reflection — The City of Rome

## Foundations Lane (K-2) — Spoken/Drawn Reflection

Draw a picture of a hill or a river you have seen — in real life, in a book, or in your imagination. Then tell a grown-up: "This is like the hills and the river by Rome!" A parent or facilitator can simply note that the student drew something and shared it out loud. Completion-based; no grade.

## Primary Lane (Grades 3-5) — Typed Reflection

In 1-2 sentences: how might Rome's hills, its river, and its peninsula shape have helped or challenged the people who first built a city there?
