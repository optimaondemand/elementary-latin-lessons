📖 TODAY'S FOCUS
Rome sits on top of hills, right next to a flowing river!
Last time we said salvē and valē. Today we go back to Rome to see WHERE it sits — on top of hills, next to a big river, inside a country shaped like a boot!

⛰️ PART 2 · ROME'S HILLS

Long, long ago, people built the city of Rōma right on top of hills. Not just one hill — many! People still talk about Rome's hills today. In Latin, the word for a hill (or a mountain) is mōns.

🏛️
Rōma
the city built on hills!
⛰️
mōns
a hill (or mountain)

🗺️ PART 2a · A BOOT-SHAPED LAND

Rome sits inside a country called Italia. Italia is shaped just like a big boot! Right next to Rome's hills runs a big river. Long ago, boats used that river to bring people and things into the city.

Italia — the boot-shaped land, with Rōma marked in gold
