# Module 01 Quick Check — Salvē! Greetings & the Roman World

> **Teacher note (not shown to students):** CPALMS codes — NM.1.1, NM.3.1, NH.6.3

## Foundations Lane (K-2) — Canvas Assignment (completion-based)

**Format:** Canvas Assignment, completion grade only (no numeric score).

**Student-facing instructions:**
"Match each picture to the Latin word it goes with. Draw a line to connect them, or tell a grown-up which ones go together out loud. There's no wrong way to try — this is just for practice!"

Present two picture cards and two word cards (mixed order) for students to pair up:

| Picture card | Latin word | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 👋 a hand waving hello | **salvē** | hello |
| 🚶 a person walking away, waving | **valē** | goodbye |

**Teacher answer key (matching pairs):** waving hand → salvē; walking away → valē.

**Grading:** Mark complete for any good-faith attempt at both pairs; no pair needs to be "correct" for credit. This is the very first module of the year — the goal is exposure and enjoyment, not accuracy.

## Primary Lane (Grades 3-5) — Canvas Quiz (auto-graded, 5 questions, multiple choice)

**Format:** Canvas Quiz, auto-graded, best of 2 attempts, 5 multiple-choice questions.

**Question 1**
What does *salvē* mean?
A) Hello *(correct)*
B) Thank you
C) Goodbye
D) Please
Feedback (correct): "Correct! Salvē means hello."

**Question 2**
What does *valē* mean?
A) Hello
B) Yes
C) No
D) Goodbye *(correct)*
Feedback (correct): "Right! Valē means goodbye."

**Question 3**
Italy is shaped like a...
A) circle
B) tall boot *(correct)*
C) square
D) triangle
Feedback (correct): "Correct! Italia is shaped like a tall boot reaching into the Mediterranean Sea."

**Question 4**
Which English word grew out of the Latin word *terra* ("earth, land")?
A) terrific
B) terminal
C) territory *(correct)*
D) terror
Feedback (correct): "Yes! Territory comes from terra, the Latin word for land."

**Question 5**
Which English word grew out of the Latin word *vīvere* ("to live")?
A) visible
B) value
C) virtue
D) survive *(correct)*
Feedback (correct): "Exactly! Survive comes from vīvere, the Latin word for 'to live.'"
