# Module 01 Reflection — Salvē! Greetings & the Roman World

## Foundations Lane (K-2) — Spoken Reflection

Say *salvē* and *valē* to a grown-up today. A parent or facilitator can simply note that the student said both words out loud. Completion-based; no grade.

## Primary Lane (Grades 3-5) — Typed Reflection

In 1-2 sentences: what surprised you about Latin today, or about a word you already knew turning out to have Latin roots?
