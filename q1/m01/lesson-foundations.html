📖 TODAY'S FOCUS
A very old language that is still all around us!
Long, long ago, people in a faraway land called Rome spoke a language called Latin. Nobody speaks it every day anymore — but guess what? Pieces of it are hiding in words YOU say every day! Today we say our first Latin words.
🎯 BY THE END OF TODAY YOU WILL BE ABLE TO…
1
I can say salvē (hello) and valē (goodbye).
2
I can point to where Rome is on a map.
3
I can show a grown-up one Latin word I learned.
📋 WHAT YOU NEED
▶
Headphones or speakers
▶
A grown-up nearby is helpful but not required

👋 PART 3 · YOUR FIRST LATIN WORDS

A Roman child said these two words every single day. Say them out loud with me!

👋
Salvē!
it means "Hello!"
🙋
Valē!
it means "Goodbye!"

Now you try: which word matches which picture?

⚡ TRY IT · MATCH THE WORD
Tap the word that matches the picture.
👋 (a hand waving hello)
🚶 (a person walking away, waving)
🤗 NEED HELP?
Two new words is plenty for one day! Play the video again and say the words along with it. You will hear salvē and valē many more times this year.
▶  Go Further: find Italy on a real map with a grown-up
If you have a globe or a map at home, ask a grown-up to help you find Italy. It really does look like a boot!
