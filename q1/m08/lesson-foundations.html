📖 TODAY'S FOCUS
Meet three special helpers from long ago!
Long, long ago, the Romans believed in many gods. Each god had one big job to do — we call that a god's domain. Today we meet three of them: a king who watched the sky, a queen who watched over families, and a god who ruled the sea!
🏛️ YOUR GUIDE · MAGISTER CAESAR
🎯 BY THE END OF TODAY YOU WILL BE ABLE TO…
1
I can point to a picture and say which god is which: Iuppiter, Iūnō, or Neptūnus.
2
I can match each god to their special symbol.
3
I can tell a grown-up one job that a god had.

▶
