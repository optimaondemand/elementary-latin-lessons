# Module 08 Reflection — Gods of Rome I

## Foundations Lane (K-2) — Spoken/Drawn Reflection
Tell a grown-up one job that a god from today's lesson had — for example, "Neptune takes care of the sea" or "Juno watches over families." This is completion-based and not graded, and it can be folded into the grown-up's dictation for the Roman Passport entry (see roman-passport-entry.md).

## Primary Lane (Grades 3-5) — Typed Reflection
In one or two typed sentences: which of the three gods' domains (sky, marriage and family, or the sea) did you find most interesting, and why? This can be typed alongside the fact and Latin word already going into this quarter's Roman Passport Page 1 entry (see roman-passport-entry.md).
