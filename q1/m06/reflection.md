# Module 06 Reflection — Roman Food & Market

## Foundations Lane (K-2) — Spoken/Drawn Reflection

**Prompt:** "Draw or tell a grown-up: what is one food you think a Roman child might have eaten? Why do you think so?"

A parent or facilitator can simply note that the student completed the prompt (a quick description of what the child said or drew is enough). Completion-based only — no numeric grade.

## Primary Lane (Grades 3-5) — Typed Reflection

**Prompt:** "In one or two sentences, describe one Roman food habit that surprised you, and compare it to something you eat today."
