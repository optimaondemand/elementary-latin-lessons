# Module 05 Reflection — A Roman Family & Home

## Foundations Lane (K-2) — Spoken/Drawn Reflection

**Prompt (completion-based, no grade):** "Draw a picture of your own family, or tell a grown-up who is in your familia. If you can, name one person out loud using a Latin word you learned today — māter, pater, fīlius, or fīlia."

*Parent/facilitator note: Simply note that the student drew or spoke their response. No scoring is needed — this is a completion check.*

## Primary Lane (Grades 3-5) — Typed Reflection

**Prompt:** "In one or two sentences, tell which room of a Roman domus you would most want in your own house, and why."
