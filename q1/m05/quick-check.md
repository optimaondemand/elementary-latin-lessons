# Module 05 Quick Check — A Roman Family & Home

> **Teacher note (not shown to students):** CPALMS codes — NM.3.4, NH.6.1

## Foundations Lane (K-2) — Canvas Assignment (completion-based)

**Format:** Canvas Assignment, completion grade only (no numeric grade).

**Student-facing instructions:**
"Look at the picture of a Roman family below. Draw a line (or drag, if you are using a computer) from each person's picture to the Latin word that names them. Then draw a line from the picture of the house to its Latin word. When you are done, click Submit — you don't need to get every match perfect to finish this activity!"

**Picture set provided to students (5 picture cards + 5 word cards, shuffled):**
- Picture of a mother
- Picture of a father
- Picture of a boy (son)
- Picture of a girl (daughter)
- Picture of a house with an open roof

**Matching-pairs answer key (for teacher):**
| Picture | Latin Word |
|---|---|
| Mother | māter |
| Father | pater |
| Boy (son) | fīlius |
| Girl (daughter) | fīlia |
| House | domus |

**Bonus picture (optional, not required for completion):** A picture of all four family members together, matched to *familia* (the whole family).

## Primary Lane (Grades 3-5) — Canvas Quiz (auto-graded, 5 questions, multiple choice)

**Question 1**
What is a *domus*?
A. A Roman marketplace
B. A well-off Roman family's house
C. A Roman school
D. A Roman ship
**Correct answer: B**
Feedback (shown for correct answer): "Right — a domus was the house of a well-off Roman family."

**Question 2**
What was the compluvium?
A. A pool of water in the atrium
B. A small bedroom
C. An opening in the atrium's roof that let in light and air
D. The front door of the house
**Correct answer: C**
Feedback (shown for correct answer): "Correct — the compluvium was the roof opening above the atrium."

**Question 3**
What happened to rainwater that fell through the compluvium?
A. It was thrown back outside
B. It collected in a shallow pool called the impluvium
C. It was carried to the market in jars
D. It flowed straight into the street
**Correct answer: B**
Feedback (shown for correct answer): "Yes — rainwater collected in the impluvium, the shallow pool below the compluvium."

**Question 4**
Which Latin word means "father," the head of a Roman household?
A. māter
B. fīlius
C. pater
D. familia
**Correct answer: C**
Feedback (shown for correct answer): "That's right — pater means father."

**Question 5**
Around what age did many Roman children begin attending lessons?
A. Age six or seven
B. Age one or two
C. Age fifteen
D. They never attended lessons
**Correct answer: A**
Feedback (shown for correct answer): "Correct — many boys, and some girls, began lessons around age six or seven."
