# Module 07 Reflection — Roman Clothing

## Foundations Lane (K-2) — Spoken/Drawn Reflection

**Format:** Completion-based, no grade. Parent/facilitator notes or watches; no independent reading or writing required.

**Prompt:** "Tell or draw for a grown-up: if you were a Roman for one special day, would you wear a toga or a tunica? Why did you pick that one?"

## Primary Lane (Grades 3-5) — Typed Reflection

**Format:** Typed response, 1-2 sentences.

**Prompt:** "In 1-2 sentences: which Roman outfit — the everyday tunica or the formal toga — would you rather have worn, and why?"
