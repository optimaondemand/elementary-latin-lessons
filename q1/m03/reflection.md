# Module 03 Reflection — Roman Numbers I (I-X)

## Foundations Lane (K-2) — Spoken/Drawn Reflection

**Prompt:** "Find or draw one Roman numeral you can spot at home — maybe on a clock, in a book, or on a remote control. Point to it (or show your drawing) and tell a grown-up what number it means."

**Format:** Spoken or drawn, completion-based — no grade. A parent or facilitator simply notes that the student pointed to or drew a Roman numeral and correctly said what quantity it represents.

## Primary Lane (Grades 3-5) — Typed Reflection

**Prompt:** "In one or two sentences, where do you think you're most likely to spot a Roman numeral in your own life — on a clock, in a book, watching sports, or somewhere else? Explain your thinking."

**Format:** Typed, 1-2 sentences, submitted as a short Canvas text entry.
