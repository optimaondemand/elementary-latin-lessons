📖 TODAY'S FOCUS
Romans didn't write numbers the way we do!
Instead of 1, 2, 3, Romans used letter shapes like I, V, and X to write numbers. And guess what — those letter-numbers are STILL hiding all around you today, on clocks and other everyday things! Today we count to ten like a Roman.
🎯 BY THE END OF TODAY YOU WILL BE ABLE TO…
1
I can say the Latin numbers one through ten.
2
I can match a Roman numeral shape to how many it means.
3
I can find a Roman numeral hiding on a clock.
