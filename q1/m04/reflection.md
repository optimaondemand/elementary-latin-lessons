# Module 04 Reflection — The Founding Story

## Foundations Lane (K-2) — Spoken/Drawn Reflection

**Type:** Spoken or drawn, completion-based, no grade.

**Prompt:** Tell a grown-up (or draw a picture of) the story of the twins and the she-wolf, in your own words. Which part was your favorite: the babies by the river, the she-wolf, or the new city?

*Parent/facilitator note: Simply initial or note that the student shared or drew the story. No scoring — completion only.*

## Primary Lane (Grades 3-5) — Typed Reflection

**Type:** Typed, 1-2 sentences.

**Prompt:** Why do you think the Romans liked telling this story about their own city? Write 1-2 sentences in your own words.
