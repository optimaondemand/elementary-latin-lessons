📖 TODAY'S FOCUS
Every place has a story about how it began.
The Romans told a story about two baby brothers, a kind wolf, and a brand-new city. It is a legend — that means it is a story people have told for a very long time, passed down instead of written down as it happened. Today we hear the legend the Romans told about themselves.

1
I can tell the story of the twins and the she-wolf using pictures.
2

3
I can put the story pictures in the right order.

🐺 PART 2 · A LEGEND, NOT A FACT

Long ago, the Romans told a story about how their city began. Nobody knows if it really happened this way — it is a legend, a story passed down for a long, long time. Here is the story they told:

👶👶🌊
Two baby brothers were left by a river.
🐺💕
A lupa (she-wolf) found the babies and cared for them.
🏛️✨
The brothers grew up and built a brand-new city — and named it Rōma.
