## Foundations Lane (K-2) — Canvas Assignment (completion-based)

**Type:** Canvas Assignment, completion grade only (no numeric grade, no rubric score).

**Student-facing instructions:**

> Put the story pictures in order! You will see 5 pictures from the legend of the twins and the she-wolf. Drag them (or number them) from 1st to 5th, in the order they happen in the story. There is no wrong way to take your time — play it as many times as you like.

**Picture cards to include (in scrambled order for the assignment):**
- 👶👶🌊 Two baby brothers are left by a river.
- 🐺💕 A lupa (she-wolf) finds the babies and cares for them.
- 🧍‍♂️🧍‍♂️ The brothers are grown up.
- 🤝🗺️ The brothers talk about where to build a city.
- 🏛️👑 Romulus names the new city Rōma.

**Correct sequence / answer key (teacher use):**
1. Two baby brothers are left by a river.
2. A lupa (she-wolf) finds the babies and cares for them.
3. The brothers are grown up.
4. The brothers talk about where to build a city.
5. Romulus names the new city Rōma.

**Grading:** Mark complete/incomplete based on submission. Do not assign a numeric grade or letter grade.

## Primary Lane (Grades 3-5) — Canvas Quiz (auto-graded, 5 questions, multiple choice)

**Type:** Canvas Quiz, auto-graded, best of 2 attempts.

**Question 1**
What does the Latin word *lupa* mean?
- A. A river
- B. A she-wolf ✅ **(correct)**
- C. A city
- D. A king
- Feedback (correct): "Right — lupa means 'she-wolf,' the animal that found the twins in the legend."

**Question 2**
What does the Latin word *urbs* mean?
- A. City ✅ **(correct)**
- B. Brother
- C. Wolf
- D. River
- Feedback (correct): "Yes — urbs means 'city,' and it's the root of English words like urban and suburb."

**Question 3**
What does the Latin word *frāter* mean?
- A. Sister
- B. Brother ✅ **(correct)**
- C. Father
- D. Friend
- Feedback (correct): "Correct — frāter means 'brother.' It's the root of the English word fraternity."

**Question 4**

- A. A king
- B. A farmer
- C. A she-wolf ✅ **(correct)**
- D. A soldier
- Feedback (correct): "That's right — the legend says a lupa (she-wolf) found and cared for the twins."

**Question 5**

- A. The brothers never spoke to each other again.
- B. Romulus became the city's first king and named the new city Rōma after himself. ✅ **(correct)**

- Feedback (correct): "Yes — Romulus became king and named the new city Rōma. This is a legend the Romans told about themselves, not proven history."
