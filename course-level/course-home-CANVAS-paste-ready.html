YOUR ROMAN PASSPORT
One Word, One Fact, One Drawing — Four Times a Year
Your Roman Passport is a running scrapbook of everything you discover about the Roman world this year. Once each quarter — at Modules 08, 16, 24, and 33 — you'll add a new page: a drawing, a fact you learned, and a Latin word you can say. At the end of the year, you'll show off your whole passport.
This is not a test — it's a keepsake. Grades K-2 dictate their page to a grown-up; Grades 3-5 write and draw it themselves in their Roman Passport document.
