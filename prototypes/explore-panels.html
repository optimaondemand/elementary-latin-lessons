Format A Illustrated Explainer — used where we have scene art
Colossēum Q2 · M14
the Colosseum — the great amphitheater of Rome

The Colosseum was the biggest arena in all of Rome — a giant oval of stone arches where whole crowds came to watch shows together. Roman builders were amazing engineers, and even today people study how they made it!

👥 Did you know?About 50,000 people could sit inside — as many as fill a big sports stadium today.
⛵ Did you know?A huge cloth sun-shade called the vēlārium could be stretched over the top to keep the crowd cool in the sun. Sailors helped pull the ropes!
⚙️ Did you know?Under the sandy floor were secret tunnels and wooden lifts that could raise scenery and animals up into the arena, like a trap door.
